About us

Septum is an e-learning platform. We’re committed to building the best learning experience inside and out, making Septum the best place for our team to learn, teach, and create the online learning experience of the future. Septum has focus on simplicity and practice easy and straight-forward learning.
Septum is a platform for all the students and instructors out there who wish to learn and teach for free. It's a platform where you as a user can help others learn by uploading video tutorials or documents about (..couldn't find exact words..) or you can also learn as a student from the instructors uploading thier material here on Septum.

Our mission

We’re not one of those e-learning websites. We are rethinking education from the bottom up. The web has rethought nearly everything - commerce, social networking, healthcare, and more. We are building the education the world needs - the first truly net native education. We take more cues from modern tech innovators in creating an engaging educational experience than we do from the classroom.
We want your full participation in making this academy the best e-learning platform.

Education is broken. Come help us build the education the world deserves.
